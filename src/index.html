Meet your instructor

Archie Jones
Instructor, course author

My name is Alex and I've been working with data for almost 10 years. I am currently employed as a manager of data analytics in the insurance industry. I used to work in a restaurant and night clubs but at some point I grew tired of it and decided to change careers. Since then, I have worked in oil & gas, international logistics and now in insurance.

At first, all I was driven by was higher salary so IT seemed like a no-brainer. However, it's not easy to get into that industry without any technical skills so I started looking for paths of least resistance and found data analytics.

Just like most people, I started with Excel and soon moved onto more specialized data tools. Looking back, I'm glad I didn't choose to go to college to get an expensive computer science degree because I spent a lot less money and time learning exactly what was needed to get an entry-level job in the field.

Now, I would like to help others get their chance to do the same!
